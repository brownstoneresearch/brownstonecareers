# Brownstone Careers Bio-Data & Identity Form Upgrade

The candidate application now includes:

- SSN last four digits only, with four-digit validation and a show/hide control.
- Mother's maiden name.
- Separate required uploads for the front and back of a government-issued ID.
- JPG, PNG, WEBP, and PDF identity-file support, limited to 5 MB per file.
- Dynamic selected-file names and file-size feedback.
- Identity-document quality guidance.
- Updated consent and privacy language.
- Backend validation and Resend attachment support for the resume and both ID files.

## Privacy note

This form intentionally requests only the final four SSN digits and warns candidates never to provide a full SSN, PIN, password, or banking credentials. Limit access to the recruitment inbox, enable multi-factor authentication, and establish a clear retention/deletion policy for identity documents.
