# Brownstone Careers Logo Placement Rebrand

This release introduces a unified executive logo-placement system across the website.

## Header

- A compact illuminated brand dock replaces the flat logo treatment.
- The complete Brownstone Careers wordmark remains clearly readable.
- A restrained cobalt/cyan accent rail reinforces the agency identity.
- Desktop, tablet, mobile, and scrolled-header dimensions are independently balanced.
- The logo remains the homepage link; no Home navigation item is added.

## Mobile navigation

- The drawer logo now sits in a dedicated glass brand stage.
- Spacing, alignment, contrast, and logo rendering are improved on small screens.

## Footer

- The logo is placed in a premium brand sanctuary with the same visual language as the header.
- Logo dimensions are larger and clearer without overwhelming the footer content.
- Mobile sizing remains proportional and readable.

## Technical notes

- Cloudflare Pages Functions and form handlers are unchanged.
- Resend, Turnstile, SEO, IndexNow, and deployment configuration are preserved.
- The source stylesheet and production build output are regenerated during `npm run build`.
