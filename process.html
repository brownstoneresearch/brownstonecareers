Hiring process

Six stages designed to evaluate readiness and fit.

Understanding the process helps you prepare thoughtful, complete, and professional responses.

01

Online Application

Provide accurate contact details, your preferred role, availability, professional experience, and technical skills.

Prepare:
  • A concise employment history
  • Your current time zone and start date
  • Relevant software and equipment information
02

Pre-Screening Questionnaire

Answer structured questions about your background, motivation, reliability, work style, and long-term goals.

Prepare:
  • Specific examples rather than general claims
  • Clear and professional written responses
  • Honest explanations of skill gaps
03

Role-Specific Assessment

Complete a practical exercise that reflects the role, such as data accuracy, customer response, administrative organization, or financial record review.

04

Recruitment Interview

Discuss your experience, availability, communication style, expectations, and suitability for remote work.

05

Verification

Where applicable, candidate information, references, equipment, identity, or eligibility may be reviewed before onboarding.

06

Offer and Onboarding

Successful candidates receive next-step documents, confidentiality requirements, orientation details, and role-specific instructions.
