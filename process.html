Hiring process

A transparent path from application to onboarding.

Every stage is designed to evaluate suitability fairly and confirm readiness for remote work.

1

Online Application

Submit your details, preferred role, availability, and professional summary.

2

Pre-Screening

Answer structured questions about experience, work style, technology, and long-term goals.

3

Skills Assessment

Complete a typing, accuracy, customer-service, administration, or finance-related exercise.

4

Interview

Selected candidates meet with recruitment to discuss qualifications and expectations.

5

Verification

Information, equipment, references, and eligibility may be verified where applicable.

6

Onboarding

Successful candidates receive agreement, confidentiality, and role onboarding documents.
