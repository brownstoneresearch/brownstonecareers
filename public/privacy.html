Applicant privacy

Your information deserves clear, responsible handling.

This notice explains the information Brownstone Careers may collect during recruitment and how it is intended to be used and protected.

Effective dateJuly 23, 2026

Questions about this notice can be sent to our official support email.

support@brownstonecareers.agency

Applicant Privacy Notice

Brownstone Careers collects information that candidates voluntarily provide when applying for opportunities, contacting support, completing screening, or participating in recruitment activities.

Information we may collect

This may include contact details, residential address, work history, role preferences, availability, professional skills, résumé information, identification files, and limited identity-verification information requested through the application process.

How information is used

Applicant information is used to review applications, assess role suitability, communicate recruitment updates, support identity or eligibility verification, prevent misuse of the platform, and maintain recruitment records.

How information is shared

Information may be shared only with authorized recruitment personnel, service providers that support secure form processing or communication, and other parties when required by law or necessary to protect candidates, Brownstone Careers, or the integrity of the recruitment process.

Sensitive information

Never submit a full Social Security number, password, PIN, complete payment-card information, or online-banking credentials. Use only the official Brownstone Careers website and official support channels.

Retention and deletion

Information should be retained only for legitimate recruitment, verification, security, and recordkeeping purposes. Candidates may contact Brownstone Careers to ask about their application information or request deletion, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.

Security

Brownstone Careers uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards; however, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure. Candidates should submit only requested information and use secure personal devices and networks.

Your choices

You may choose not to provide optional information. Required application information is identified on the form. Declining to provide required information may prevent completion of the applicable recruitment step.

Updates to this notice

This notice may be updated as recruitment practices, technology, or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the latest published version.
