Recruitment process

A clear recruitment process from application to onboarding.

Understand each stage before you apply so you can prepare strong, accurate, and professional responses.

6-stepjourneyClearscreening stagesProfessionalonboarding prep

Online Application

Submit your details, preferred role, availability, resume, and professional summary.

02

Pre-Screening

Answer structured questions about experience, work style, technology, reliability, and goals.

03

Skills Assessment

Complete a role-relevant exercise such as typing, accuracy, customer response, admin organization, or finance review.

Verification

Information, equipment, references, and eligibility may be checked where applicable.

06

Onboarding

Successful candidates receive agreement, confidentiality, orientation, and role onboarding documents.

Assessment focus

What the recruiting team may review.

Assessments vary by role, but most remote opportunities require accuracy, communication, organization, and responsible handling of information.

Typing & accuracy

Speed, precision, formatting, and attention to detail.

Customer response

Professional tone, empathy, issue handling, and written clarity.

Administration

Task organization, scheduling logic, documentation, and follow-through.

Finance support

Basic record review, reconciliation thinking, and confidentiality.

Before you apply

Prepare your candidate materials.

A complete application gives recruiters a better view of your skills and readiness.

  • Updated resume or CV in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format.
  • Reliable email address and phone number.
  • Clear role preference and available start date.
  • Specific examples of relevant work experience.
  • Details about your computer, internet, workspace, and availability.
