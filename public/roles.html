Finance Administrator

Support invoicing, reconciliation, reporting, records, and confidential finance administration with accuracy and discretion.

Typical responsibilities

  • Maintain invoices, transaction logs, expense records, and supporting documents.
  • Assist with account, payment, and bank reconciliation tasks.
  • Prepare routine summaries and identify inconsistencies for review.
  • Protect confidential financial information and follow internal controls.

Who may be a strong fit

Detail-oriented candidates with bookkeeping, spreadsheet, QuickBooks, accounts-support, or office finance experience.

Data Entry Specialist

Enter, verify, organize, and update business information so teams can rely on clean records and consistent data quality.

Typical responsibilities

  • Enter information into approved systems, spreadsheets, or databases.
  • Review entries for spelling, formatting, duplicates, and missing details.
  • Organize digital files and maintain structured records.
  • Follow data security and privacy expectations.

Who may be a strong fit

Candidates who type accurately, follow instructions carefully, and can maintain focus on repetitive detail-heavy work.

Customer Service Representative

Deliver professional support through clear communication, issue tracking, documentation, and respectful customer follow-through.

Typical responsibilities

  • Respond to customer or client inquiries through assigned channels.
  • Document conversations, actions, and next steps accurately.
  • Escalate complex issues with useful notes and context.
  • Maintain polite, calm, and solution-focused communication.

Who may be a strong fit

Patient communicators with email, chat, phone, helpdesk, hospitality, sales support, or client-facing experience.

Administrative Assistant

Coordinate schedules, files, inboxes, documents, and operational tasks that keep remote teams organized and productive.

  • Support calendars, scheduling, reminders, and team coordination.
  • Manage routine inbox, follow-up, and administrative requests.
  • Track tasks and maintain reliable communication with team members.

Who may be a strong fit

Organized candidates with office support, calendar management, documentation, virtual assistance, or operations experience.
