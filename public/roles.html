Finance Administrator

Support invoicing, reconciliation, expense records, reporting, document control, and confidential finance administration with accuracy and discretion.

Typical responsibilities

  • Maintain invoices, transaction logs, receipts, expense records, and supporting documents.
  • Assist with account, payment, and bank reconciliation tasks.
  • Prepare routine summaries, flag inconsistencies, and support finance reporting.
  • Protect confidential financial information and follow internal controls.

Who may be a strong fit

Detail-oriented candidates with bookkeeping, spreadsheet, QuickBooks, accounts-support, office finance, or reconciliation experience.

Data Entry Specialist

Enter, verify, clean, organize, and update business information so remote teams can rely on accurate records and consistent data quality.

Typical responsibilities

  • Enter information into approved systems, spreadsheets, CRMs, or databases.
  • Review entries for spelling, formatting, duplicates, missing details, and incorrect categories.
  • Organize digital files, name documents correctly, and maintain clean record trails.
  • Follow privacy, data security, and quality-control expectations.

Who may be a strong fit

Candidates who type accurately, follow instructions carefully, and can maintain focus on repetitive detail-heavy work.

Customer Service Representative

Deliver professional email, chat, helpdesk, or phone support through clear communication, issue tracking, documentation, and respectful follow-through.

Typical responsibilities

  • Respond to customer or client inquiries through assigned channels.
  • Document conversations, actions, promises, and next steps accurately.
  • Escalate complex issues with useful notes and context.
  • Maintain polite, calm, solution-focused communication under pressure.

Who may be a strong fit

Patient communicators with email, chat, call-center, helpdesk, hospitality, sales support, or client-facing experience.

Administrative Assistant

Coordinate schedules, inboxes, documents, files, task lists, and operational follow-up so remote teams stay organized and productive.

Typical responsibilities

Virtual Assistant

Support busy professionals and teams with remote coordination, online research, inbox assistance, appointment management, and task follow-through.

Typical responsibilities

Recruitment Coordinator

Assist recruitment teams with candidate records, interview scheduling, applicant communication, screening notes, and hiring pipeline organization.

Typical responsibilities

  • Organize candidate records, screening notes, and application status updates.
