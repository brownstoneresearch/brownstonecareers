Candidate FAQ

Answers that help you apply with confidence.

Review common questions about remote roles, assessments, equipment, application safety, and recruitment communication.

FastanswersSafeapplication guidanceRemotework clarity
Need individual support?

Contact our recruitment support team.

Use the official support email or WhatsApp link shown on this website. Include your full name, role of interest, and a clear question.

Contact support
Are the advertised roles remote?

The website focuses on remote-friendly opportunities. Exact working hours, location eligibility, and role arrangements are confirmed during recruitment.

Do I need direct experience?

Direct experience is helpful, but relevant transferable skills, strong assessment results, reliability, and trainability may also support your application.

What equipment should I have?

Requirements depend on the role, but candidates may need a reliable computer, stable internet connection, webcam, headset, and a quiet workspace.

How are assessment results communicated?

Recruitment updates are sent to the email address provided during application. Keep your contact information accurate and monitor your inbox.

Is there an application fee?

No legitimate Brownstone Careers application requires an upfront application fee. Do not share passwords, PINs, complete card details, or online-banking credentials.

How should I choose a role?

Select the role that most closely reflects your strongest and most recent experience. Tailor your professional summary to that role.

What makes an application stand out?

Clear examples, accurate information, professional communication, role-specific skills, and evidence of reliability make an application more persuasive.

Can I apply for more than one role?

Choose the strongest fit first. If your background supports multiple pathways, mention that in your professional experience or select Other when appropriate.
