Finance Administrator

Support accurate financial operations through disciplined recordkeeping, reconciliation, reporting, and confidential administrative work.

Typical responsibilities

  • Maintain invoices, transaction logs, expense records, and supporting documents.
  • Assist with bank, account, and payment reconciliations.
  • Prepare routine financial summaries and flag inconsistencies for review.
  • Protect confidential financial information and follow internal controls.

Who may be a strong fit

Best suited to detail-oriented candidates with bookkeeping, office finance, QuickBooks, spreadsheet, or accounts-support experience.

Data Entry Specialist

Turn information into reliable records by entering, validating, organizing, and updating data with speed and precision.

Typical responsibilities

  • Enter information into spreadsheets, databases, and internal systems.
  • Review records for completeness, consistency, and formatting accuracy.
  • Correct discrepancies and escalate unclear or missing information.
  • Maintain organized digital files and meet productivity standards.

Who may be a strong fit

Ideal for candidates who are focused, consistent, comfortable with repetitive tasks, and committed to high accuracy.

Customer Service Representative

Create reassuring customer experiences through professional communication, careful listening, documentation, and solution-focused support.

Typical responsibilities

  • Respond to customer questions through approved support channels.
