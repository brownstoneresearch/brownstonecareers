# Brownstone Careers Resend Sending-Domain Setup

## Recommended sending identity

Use a dedicated transactional subdomain:

- Sending domain: `mail.brownstonecareers.agency`
- From identity: `Brownstone Careers <notifications@mail.brownstonecareers.agency>`
- Reply-to inbox: `support@brownstonecareers.agency`
- Recruitment inbox: your authorized recruitment/support mailbox

The From address is used for delivery and brand reputation. Candidate replies are directed to the real support inbox through `EMAIL_REPLY_TO`.

## Resend dashboard steps

1. Open **Resend → Domains → Add Domain**.
2. Enter `mail.brownstonecareers.agency`.
3. Copy the SPF and DKIM records generated by Resend into the DNS provider for `brownstonecareers.agency`.
4. Add the optional DMARC record shown or recommended by Resend after checking any DMARC policy already configured for the root domain.
5. Wait until Resend reports the subdomain as **Verified**.
6. Do not invent or reuse DNS values from another domain. Use the exact records displayed in the Brownstone Careers Resend account.

## Cloudflare Pages variables

Under **Workers & Pages → Brownstone Careers → Settings → Variables and Secrets**, configure production and preview as appropriate:

```text
RESEND_API_KEY=<new secret Resend API key>
EMAIL_FROM=Brownstone Careers <notifications@mail.brownstonecareers.agency>
EMAIL_REPLY_TO=support@brownstonecareers.agency
RECRUITMENT_EMAIL=<authorized recruitment inbox>
TURNSTILE_SECRET_KEY=<Cloudflare Turnstile secret>
```

Redeploy after saving the variables.

## Logo used by every template

All candidate confirmations and internal website notifications use this public hosted asset:

```text
https://www.brownstonecareers.agency/assets/brownstone-logo-dark.png
```

The earlier `/assets/logo.png` reference was invalid in this project and has been removed. Some email clients initially hide remote images until the recipient chooses to display them; the email still contains the official linked logo and accessible Brownstone Careers alternative text.

## Verification

After deployment:

1. Open `/api/health` and confirm `emailConfigured` is `true`.
2. Confirm `dedicatedSendingSubdomain` is `true` on Cloudflare Pages.
3. Submit one test contact form and one test application.
4. Verify the team notification and candidate confirmation both show the same official header, logo, footer, and security notice.
5. In Resend, confirm the From address ends in `@mail.brownstonecareers.agency` and delivery events show SPF and DKIM authentication.

## Important distinction

The website can brand every email it sends, including internal notifications received by the Brownstone team. It cannot force an external sender's independent email client to include Brownstone branding. Incoming email processing through Resend requires a separate receiving-domain and webhook setup and is not required for the website forms.
