# Candidate Identity and Work History Upgrade

Version 5.5 adds the following required application fields:

- SSN last four digits only
- Mother’s maiden name
- Full current residential address
- Years of working experience
- Most recent job title
- Most recent employer
- Employment period
- Detailed working experience and achievements
- Front and back government-issued ID upload

All fields are validated by the Cloudflare Pages Function and included in the recruiter email. The ID files remain limited to JPG, PNG, WEBP, or PDF at 5 MB each.

Sensitive-data note: use appropriate access controls, retention limits, privacy notices, and legal review before collecting identity information in production.
