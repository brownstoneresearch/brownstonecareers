FAQ

Answers to common applicant questions.

Review this information before submitting your application.

Are the positions remote?

Most opportunities are remote-friendly. Exact working arrangements are confirmed during recruitment.

Is there a fee to apply?

No legitimate application requires an application fee. Do not share passwords, PINs, or sensitive banking credentials.

How will I receive my result?

Assessment and recruitment updates are sent to the email address entered on your application.

What equipment may be required?

A reliable computer, stable internet connection, webcam, and headset may be required depending on the role.

Does the Finance Administrator role require experience?

Relevant bookkeeping, invoicing, QuickBooks, Excel, reconciliation, or financial administration experience is preferred. Candidates must demonstrate confidentiality and attention to detail.

How can I contact support?

Email support@brownstonecareers.agency or message +1 (302) 534-2829 on WhatsApp.
